The Menu

Our tasting menu features 8 to 10 thoughtfully crafted courses, built around proteins from both land and sea, along with seasonal vegetables, fruits, grains, and nuts. The menu evolves regularly based on what’s fresh and in season, and every dish is prepared with precision and purpose.

The full experience typically lasts between 2 and 2½ hours.



Wine & Drinks

A curated wine pairing is available to complement the flow of the tasting menu. We also offer a focused selection of wines by the glass or bottle, along with classic cocktails and a small, considered beer list.

Our team is happy to help you choose the right pairing.



Dietary Restrictions & Substitutions

Due to the nature of our menu, we’re unable to make substitutions for personal preferences or general aversions. However, with at least 72 hours’ notice, we can accommodate certain dietary restrictions and mild allergies, including:

  • Seafood allergies
  • Tree nut/peanut allergies
  • Pregancy-related restrictions
  • Pescatarian
  • Lacto-ovo vegetarian diets (please note: egg, gelatin, and dairy cannot be removed)

These accommodations must be requested in advance via the pre-visit questionnaire sent after your reservation. Unfortunately, we cannot make last-minute changes on the day of your visit.



Allergen Advisory

Please note that while we take great care in handling allergens, we cannot guarantee an entirely allergen-free environment. Cross-contact may occur in our kitchen or through our supply chain. If you or a guest in your party has a severe allergy that could be triggered by trace amounts, we recommend reaching out to us prior to booking.



Private Dining & Events

For larger groups or more private experiences, we offer a dedicated dining room that can accommodate up to 12 guests. Full restaurant buyouts are available upon request. We also offer a neighboring event space that seats 8 to 80 guests (or up to 100 standing), perfect for receptions or special gatherings.

For more information, please contact our events team.
