Private Dining & Events

We offer several thoughtfully designed spaces for private dinners, receptions, and special events, all located within our building in the heart of Chicago’s West Side.

Whether you're hosting an intimate celebration or a large-scale gathering, our team is dedicated to creating a seamless experience built around exceptional food, atmosphere, and service.

Private Dining Rooms

Inside the restaurant, we offer two private rooms:

  • The Twelve: seats up to 12 guests
  • The Twenty: seats up to 20 guests

The full dining room is also available for exclusive buyouts.



The Studio

Our most versatile space, located just next door, ideal for:

  • Wedding receptions & ceremonies
  • Rehearsal dinners & milestone celebrations
  • Corporate gatherings & team dinners

Seated dinners: up to 100 guest
Receptions: up to 150 guests



The Bar

An intimate and elevated setting for cocktail receptions or private dinners.
Seated: up to 50 guests
Reception-style: up to 100 guests



Rooftop Terrace

An open-air space with sweeping views of the Chicago skyline.
Perfect for spring and summer receptions.
Reception-style: up to 90 guests (weather permitting)
