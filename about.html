About The Line

This is a restaurant built on obsession, the kind that starts with the smallest detail and ends with a plate designed to be remembered.

Founded by a team of chefs and creatives with deep roots in Chicago’s kitchens, our space is dedicated to precision, discipline, and hospitality at the highest level.

Dining with us is more than a meal, it’s a 2½-hour experience designed to engage every sense. From the energy of the kitchen to the rhythm of service, every element is considered. Every second counts.

For those who appreciate the craft. For those who live for the plate.
